The Journey

Building the Network

From 2 farms to 0. A commitment that grows deeper every year.

2019

The First Two

Ember & Root opens with just two farm partners: Asama Farm (tomatoes & vegetables), and a small mushroom forager in Tateshina. Kenji visits each farm weekly.

2020

Growing Together

Despite pandemic challenges, the farm network grows to 3 partners. Shinshu Pork Collective joins, providing heritage-breed pork for The Ember pizza.

2022

The 120km Promise

Kenji formalizes the distance principle: nothing travels more than 120 kilometers. The team begins mapping every ingredient's origin.

2024

0 Partners

The network reaches 0 family farms across Nagano Prefecture. From apple orchards to wheat fields, from mushroom forests to dairy pastures—every ingredient now has a local home.

2025

Beyond Pizza

Ember & Root launches educational farm visits for local schools. The next generation learns where real food comes from.

Our Commitment

We will never source from outside Nagano Prefecture unless the ingredient literally cannot grow here. And even then, we'll ask you first.
