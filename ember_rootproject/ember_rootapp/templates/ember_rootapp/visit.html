Getting Here

From anywhere in Nagano

By Train

5 minute walk from Karuizawa Station (Hokuriku Shinkansen). Exit north, follow the path toward Kumoba Pond.

By Car

Limited parking available (4 spaces). Additional parking at Kumoba Pond lot (¥500, 3 min walk).

By Bicycle

Bike racks available. Karuizawa is very bike-friendly — rentals available at the station.

Before You Come

Everything you need to know

Do you take reservations?
No — we're first come, first served. The oven only makes so much dough each night. Come early, especially on weekends.
Can I order takeout?
Yes! Click "ORDER" in the navigation to place a pickup order. We'll have it ready in 20-25 minutes.
Is the space kid-friendly?
Yes! Kids love watching the oven. We have high chairs and a simple margherita for little ones.
Do you have outdoor seating?
Yes — 8 seats on our covered patio overlooking the garden. Perfect for summer evenings.
