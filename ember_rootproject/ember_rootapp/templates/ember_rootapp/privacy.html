Introduction

At Ember & Root ("we," "our," "us"), we believe that privacy is like a perfectly fermented dough—it requires the right conditions, patience, and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, place an order, or interact with our services.

We operate in compliance with Japan's Act on the Protection of Personal Information (APPI) and align with international best practices. Just as we source ingredients within 120 kilometers of Karuizawa, we limit data collection to what's strictly necessary.

Our Promise: We will never sell your personal information. Ever. Not to anyone. Not for any reason.

Information We Collect

We collect only the information needed to serve you pizza and communicate about your order. Think of it like our ingredient list—transparent and minimal.

No Sensitive Data: We do not collect sensitive information such as government IDs, financial data (payments processed securely by third parties), or health information.

How We Use Your Information

Every piece of data has a purpose—like every ingredient in our kitchen. Here's how we use your information:

  • Process Orders: Prepare your pizza and manage pickup
  • Communicate: Send order confirmations and updates
  • Improve Service: Analyze usage to enhance our website
  • Legal Compliance: Fulfill legal obligations under APPI
  • Fraud Prevention: Protect against unauthorized activity

Legal Basis for Processing (APPI)

Under Japan's APPI, we process your personal data based on the following legal grounds:

  • Consent: For newsletter subscriptions and optional communications
  • Contract Performance: To fulfill your pizza orders
  • Legal Obligation: To comply with Japanese law
  • Legitimate Interest: To improve our services and prevent fraud

Important: Our payment processors are PCI-DSS compliant. We never store your full payment information on our servers.

Security Measures

We protect your data like we protect our 900°F oven—with serious care and multiple safeguards:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Access Control: Strict employee access protocols
  • Secure Storage: Encrypted databases with regular backups
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring and threat detection
  • Regular Audits: Quarterly security assessments

Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as a good pizza stays hot—just long enough to serve its purpose:

  • Order Information: 3 years for transaction records (legal requirement)
  • Account Information: Until you request deletion
  • Analytics Data: 14 months (anonymized)

Your Rights (Under APPI)

Japan's APPI gives you specific rights over your personal information:

Right to Access

Request a copy of your data

Right to Rectification

Correct inaccurate information

Right to Deletion

Request data erasure

Right to Restrict

Limit how we use your data

To exercise any of these rights, contact our Data Protection Officer using the information in the Contact section below. We will respond within 30 days as required by APPI.

Children's Privacy

Ember & Root does not knowingly collect information from children under 16 years of age. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will delete such information promptly.

International Data Transfers

Our servers are located in Japan. If you are accessing our website from outside Japan, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in Japan where our central database operates. By using our services, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally—like our seasonal menu, it evolves. When we make changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Notify newsletter subscribers of material changes
  • Post a notice on our website for 30 days

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
