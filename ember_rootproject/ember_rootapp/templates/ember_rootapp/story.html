The Firekeeper
"My grandmother in Kyoto taught me that food is memory. Every ingredient carries the story of where it grew."

Kenji Matsumoto spent eight years in Tokyo's Michelin-starred kitchens before moving to Karuizawa. "I was cooking ingredients flown in from Europe. It felt dishonest."

In 2019, he bought an abandoned soba shop and built his oven by hand—900 bricks, six months, countless burns. Ember & Root opened that November with two pizzas and a simple promise: nothing travels more than 120 kilometers.

Today, Kenji still visits every farm personally. He still makes the dough at 5:30 each morning. And he still believes that the best ingredients are the ones you can drive to.

— Kenji Matsumoto, Founder
